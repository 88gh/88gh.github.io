About Assoc. Prof. Asano

Profile

Assoc. Prof. Yasuhiro Asano specializes in the theory of condensed matter physics, with a particular focus on superconductivity and mesoscopic transport phenomena. His research includes in-depth studies on Andreev reflection, the proximity effect, and the Josephson effect.

Education

1995 Ph.D., "Magnetoresistance in Nanoscale Magnetic Structures" (Nagoya Univ.)
